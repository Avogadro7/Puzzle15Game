## How to Play

The goal is simple: arrange all numbers in order from 1 to 15, with the empty space at the bottom right.

### Rules:
1. **Tap tiles** adjacent to the empty space to slide them
2. **Arrange numbers** from 1 to 15 in sequential order
3. **Complete the puzzle** with minimum moves and time
4. **Win** when all numbers are in correct positions!

## Game Strategy

### Tips for Success:
- **Start with corners** - Position corner numbers first
- **Work row by row** - Complete top rows before bottom
- **Plan ahead** - Think several moves in advance
- **Practice** - The more you play, the better you get!

### Difficulty Levels:
- **Beginner:** 50+ moves
- **Intermediate:** 30-50 moves
- **Expert:** Under 30 moves
- **Master:** Under 20 moves!

The 15 puzzle was invented in 1874 and became a worldwide craze. The puzzle consists of 15 numbered tiles in a 4Ã—4 grid with one empty space. The goal is to arrange the tiles in numerical order by sliding them into the empty space.
